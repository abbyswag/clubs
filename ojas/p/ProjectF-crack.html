TRANSMISSION TOWER AND INSULATOR CRACK DETECTION

Transmission of electricity through overhead transmission lines is a widely used method for power transmission from one location to another. Failure is a critical issue in this essential service. The location of the fault must be identified for recovery from the failure. Though there is human effort involved in fault detection, technology assisted solutions can save time and resources. Therefore the proposed project uses computer vision and machine learning to identify the faults and their respective locations.

    OBJECTIVES :

  • Prediction and analysis of Small scale defects and Chipped/ broken insulators.
  • Detection of cracks in transmission towers and poles.
