Team Vibhav

Team Vibhav is the Departmental Team of Electronics & Communication Engineering Department which works for Nimbus-Annual Technical Festival of National Institute of Technology, Hamirpur. Team Vibhav nurtures intense and genuine commitment. We have a diverse pool of students who are constantly trying to innovate and push the boundaries. We at Team Vibhav believe that technology can provide optimum solutions to the problems faced by the society. We make projects which are challenging and aim to tackle problems that are prevalent in our society.

Projects

The world needs innovation and ingenuity. Thereby, don't let your ideas die out! Get your brain buzzing and let your ideas become a revolution. With this ambition, Team Vibhav has fabricated some exceptional ideas into awe-inspiring projects. Whether it be a simulated 3-D environment of the NITH campus, a virtual domain for exhibition or real-time spawning of 2-D depictions, the inventive minds of Team Vibhav have shown utmost valour by turning insensible concepts into reality. Using technology to save our mother nature or helping a marginalized section of society, we stand strong to impart these ideas into action. In this period of turmoil, Team Vibhav also worked for relief in healthcare. Additionally, we carried out many worthwhile projects that shape the life of a common man in a better and advanced way, owning a vast expanse of application that ranges from usage at home to a big shopping mall and help students recognize their potential by making their education much effective and interactive. Application of knowledge and contributing to the nation-building process as model citizens is our duty. Owing to this fact, Team Vibhav showcases ten bewildering projects.
